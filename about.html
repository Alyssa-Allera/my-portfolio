About Me

A graduate of Polytechnic University of the Philippines with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Alyssa has worked alongside various individuals as a freelance digital artist. Using social media platforms as a means of advertisment and transaction, they have been self-employed for a good 4 years.

Going by the alias of Yamato, this artist has always been passionate about designing and making art for both themselves and for their client ever since they have learned how digital art is made, striving through the tough freelancing market with the determination to satisfy the request of their client.
